Who is Limco Consulting and Management?


Limco Consulting and Management is a 100% Black Economic Empowerment company founded in 2005, The company is led by team of specialist who are passionate about skills development with extensive and global experience, Limco works with different Government Departments and corporate institutions to build relationships aimed at reducing unemployment through skills development and identify skills shortage within our country

ACCREDITATION

At Limco Consulting & Management we believe each client is unique and deserves a dedicated service. We guarantee excellent and enhanced delivery.
