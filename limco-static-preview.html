About Limco

Who Is Limco Consulting and Management?

Limco Consulting and Management is a 100% Black Economic Empowerment company founded in 2005. The company is led by specialists who are passionate about skills development and institutional delivery.

We work with government departments, SETAs, municipalities, public institutions and corporate organisations to reduce unemployment, address scarce skills, strengthen workplace readiness and support long-term economic participation.
